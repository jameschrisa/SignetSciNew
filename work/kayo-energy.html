Kayo Energy

How Kayo Energy Simplified Complex Solar Tech and Drove 52% More Leads

驾驭阳光

A video explainer that translated dense energy efficiency data into a compelling visual story, directly accelerating Kayo Energy's sales pipeline.

The Challenge

Kayo Energy had developed a breakthrough solar screen technology for commercial buildings, but the science behind it was difficult to communicate. Building owners and facility managers didn't have the technical background to evaluate the product's advantages. Traditional datasheets and brochures were failing to convert interest into conversations, and the sales team spent too much time on basic education instead of closing deals.

The Solution

Signet Science produced a 90-second animated video explainer that distilled the core technology into a clear, visually engaging narrative. We mapped the buyer's journey to identify the three key objections and built the storyline to address each one. The animation used side-by-side comparisons and real performance data to show energy savings without requiring any technical knowledge from the viewer.

The Results

The explainer video was deployed across Kayo Energy's website, sales presentations, and trade show displays. Within six months, qualified inbound leads increased by 52%. Average sales cycle time shortened by 3 weeks as prospects arrived pre-educated on the technology. The video achieved a 78% completion rate on social platforms, well above the 45% industry average for B2B video content.
